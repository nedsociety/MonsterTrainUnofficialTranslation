[h1][b][u]IMPORTANT[/u][/b] - How to apply?[/h1]
[olist]
    [*]Subscribe to the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2187468759]Mod Loader[/url] before subscribing to any other mods, including this.

    [*]Subscribe to this mod.

    [*]Start the game.

    [*]Check: The Mod Settings menu should have [b]MonsterTrainUnofficialTranslation[/b] mod active.

    [*]Check: Make sure if you have English set as your language (if you have already changed the language before).
    [url=https://i.imgur.com/8VSz217.png]#Image step[/url]

    [*]Press F1, click the MonsterTrainUnofficialTranslation, then change the language to your liking.
    [url=https://i.imgur.com/KFW1HSB.png]#Image step[/url]

    [*]Restart the game.
[/olist]
